Opportunities

Funded graduate student position in climate dynamics in the Department of Geosciences at the University of Arizona (starting fall 2021). See ad for more details.

The successful applicant will analyze climate model output of South American hydroclimate through the Holocene. State-of-the-art climate model simulations with moisture tracking capabilities will be utilized to explore the origin of precipitation and assess how transport pathways are modulated by major climate modes and across climate states. Students with a BSc or MSc in atmospheric and/or ocean sciences, geosciences, physics, or math are especially encouraged to apply.

Applications (link to application portal) must be received by January 8th for fall 2021 admission. Prospective applicants are encouraged to contact Dr. Marcus Lofverstrom for further information.
